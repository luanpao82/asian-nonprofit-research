About This Project

This database presents a comprehensive text analysis of nonprofit research article titles across eight Asian countries: Bangladesh, China, Japan, Korea, Nepal, Taiwan, Turkey, and Vietnam.

Methodology

Data Collection

Research articles were collected from each country's local academic databases and bibliographic sources. Article titles were translated to English where the original language was not English. The dataset comprises over 16,000 articles spanning from 1984 to 2026.

Text Analysis

All text was preprocessed by:

  1. Converting to lowercase
  2. Removing non-alphabetic tokens
  3. Lemmatizing words
  4. Collapsing synonyms (e.g., nonprofit / non-profit / nonprofits)
  5. Removing English stopwords
  6. Removing domain-generic terms (e.g., study, research, analysis, method)

Analysis Components

For each country, the following analyses are provided:

  • Publication trend — Papers by year
  • Unigram frequency — Top 25 most frequent single words
  • Bigram frequency — Top 30 two-word phrases
  • Co-occurrence pairs — Words appearing together within the same title
