Method

Methodology

Topic Similarity (Cosine): For each country, an LDA model produces topic-term probability vectors over a unified vocabulary. Cosine similarity is computed between all topic pairs across two countries. Country-level similarity = symmetrized average of maximum per-topic matches: for each topic in Country A, the maximum cosine similarity to any topic in Country B is found, averaged in both directions.

Vocabulary Overlap (Jaccard): The Jaccard index of the top 20 most frequent unigrams between each country pair. Jaccard = |A ∩ B| / |A ∪ B|, ranging from 0 (no overlap) to 1 (identical sets).

Minimum threshold: Countries with fewer than 50 valid documents are excluded from similarity analysis.
